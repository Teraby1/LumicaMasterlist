About World

FAR OUT IN THE COSMOS, FLUFFY CREATURES RESIDE IN A MYSTICAL WORLD…

Lumicas, or Lumis for short, are a nocturnal species that resemble fluffy, moth-like creatures on Planet Aether! They tend to be docile and show astounding levels of intelligence, forming bonds, creating literature and even making their own community! Their biology is incredibly flexible, allowing them to drastically change appearance depending on what elements they come in contact with!

⟢ PLANET CODE 4371-134 - AETHER

Planet 4371-134, known as Aether to its inhabitants, is a bustling planet with large amounts of intelligent wildlife! It is said that the planet was born from the fragmented remains of a larger body, resulting in large differences in environmental conditions! Aether currently has 4 discovered continents: Ambivia, the Drylands, the Wetlands and the Frostlands. Each land mass has its own deity: a powerful entity with exceptional abilities. These deities oversee their land and the inhabitants within them.

⟢ AMBIVIA - LAND OF GENEROSITY

Ambivia is one of the 4 currently known continents of Aether. The climate is quite temperate overall, rarely growing too hot in the summer but experiencing snow during its winters! Regarded primarily for its large, dense forest and abundant generosity, Ambivia is home to a wide variety of woodland species that live together under the watchful presence of Ambi. With the variety of new faces and celebrations you'll come across, there's always something to explore in Ambi's domain! Who knows - bring them an offering and you may find yourself blessed...

Lumicas


Lumis have incredibly flexible biology that allows them to inhabit almost every corner of the planet!
It's rather simple: come in contact with important enough stimuli and adapt! Many conditions have been known to trigger changes, such as harsh cold, exposure to deep water and... trail mix? These rapid adaptations, commonly known as mutations, can result in a varety of physical alteration, from an extra pair of wings to turning limbs into plant matter, like one of our mascots Jax!

WOODLAND

Anatomy

The subspecies pictured above is Woodland variant!
Woodland Lumis are usually passive, residing within the protects of Ambi in Ambivia. They tend to be fluffy with soft mammalian tails! They can naturally display any land mammal traits, such as ears, horns or hooves without the use of any item!

Anatomy

The subspecies pictured above is SALTWATER variant!
These fishy Lumis reside in the ocean and seas on Planet Aether! They're carnivorous and... wear bubbles? They can naturally display any aquatic traits, such as gills, fins or scales without the use of any item!
